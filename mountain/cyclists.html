Cyclists

Our Get On Your Bike project has been a great success. You don’t need to drive to enjoy our services. We love to cycle and would like to help you get back on two wheels. Have a look at how we’ve teamed up with our local mountain-biking club to keep them on track.

We love to cycle as much as we love to drive. We’re strong supporters of the cycle to work scheme and we want to ensure that cyclists are as welcome taking at pit-stop here as much as any driver. Our team of cyclists will take good care of you at our bicycle service station for a quick-fix to any unexpected problems. We provide sheltered parking, cycling paths and equipment for cyclists.

Here’s a list of just some of the bicycle repair services we provide in our bicycle service station:

  • Puncture (includes standard replacement inner tube)
  • Service (strip, clean, grease and adjust)
  • Fit new tyre
  • Service headset (strip, clean, grease and adjust)
  • Service brakes (check pad wear, clean and lubricate parts, lubricate cables, adjust)
  • Bleed hydraulic disc brakes and adjust (front and rear)
  • Fit new disc brake rotor
  • Service gears (check chain wear, clean and lubricate chain, moving parts, cables and adjust)
  • Adjust gear indexing
  • Replace chain
  • Suspensions: We can arrange servicing of your suspension unit, please contact us for further information on this service.

All other work, including fitting new parts, will be charged based on the amount of time it takes rather than having a fixed price for each item.
