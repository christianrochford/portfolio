Services

Our team of staff work hard to bring you the best combination of services. Whether you need to refuel your vehicle or refuel yourself, we've got what you need to make your journey more enjoyable

Fuel

We’ve got 10 unleaded petrol and diesel pumps. We also have 5 separate pumps for heavy goods vehicles. We provide biofuels because we know you care about your environment as much as we do. All of our pumps are available 24/7 and allow you to pay at the pump via laser or credit card. We also care for your car’s inner beauty so we stock the finest choice in engine oils.

Car Wash & Service

We believe that a small business should support the community that supports them. We’ve replaced that big ugly car wash machine with a team of great folk who want to get back to the workforce and do an incredible job at cleaning cars, inside and out. Our team will make your car smile. Have a look at the great services provided by our staff to make your car look new again.

Cyclists

We love to cycle as much as we love to drive. So much so that we’ve teamed up with our local cycling club to support their race days. We want to ensure that cyclists are as welcome taking a pit-stop here as much as any driver. Our team of cyclists will take good care of you at our bicycle service station for a quick-fix to any unexpected problems. Check out our sheltered bicycle parking for when you want to take a break from the road.

Food & Drinks

Have you tried a coffee from our top class barista? How about a healthy snack from our diner? We only use organic and free range produce sourced from local farmers. Have a freshly baked pastry from our bakery while reading today’s paper and make yourself at home. If we get to know you we’ll reward you with a bit more to satisfy your appetite.
