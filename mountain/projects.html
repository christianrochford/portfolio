Projects

Mountain Service Station has actively sponsored the Mountain Road Cycling Club since 2005. We’ve provided its cyclists with the team-kit and some of our great staff are members of the club, which has 42 members and has been operating for eighteen years.

Its activities are focused on mountain-biking, with group outings on Sundays, varying the routes based on specific types of training. Members also participate in popular races throughout the country and we have recently supported some of the club’s members in travelling overseas to join the European Mountain-Biking Championship. Our interest in sponsoring sports, and in this case mountain-biking, came about from this collaboration.

The number of participants in this sport increases year after year. For this reason we affirm our commitment to mountain-biking in 2012 with our contribution to funds for the building of a new centre for the Mountain Road Cycling Club. The experience gained from our collaboration with the club for so many years means we have a first-hand understanding of the world of mountain-biking and cyclists’ requirements, which has led to the creation of our bicycle service centre, with the most modern facilities and the best services to meet the needs of the most demanding cyclists.
