Classes

Instruments

We provide classes for children and adults in the following:

  • Fiddle
  • Flute
  • Tin Whistle
  • Uilleann Pipes
  • Button Accordion
  • Concertina
  • Harp
  • Banjo & Mandolin
  • Traditional Guitar Accompaniment
  • Bodhrán
  • Traditional Singing
  • Sean nós dancing

Classes for children and adults take place on Saturday mornings from 9:00 AM to 11:30 AM and on Thursday evenings from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM in Marino College, Marino Mart, Dublin 3 (opposite Fairview Park). For further information and to book contact us. See map below.

What to expect

All our teachers have been selected on the basis of their strong playing and teaching capabilities. Class sizes are small (usually 2 students per class) and our teaching programmes are designed to encourage excellence in playing technique and personal performance as well as an understanding and appreciation of the music tradition.

Student development

Children

Typically students progress to our award-winning groups and ceili bands where they get the chance to play in festivals and compete in national competitions.

The student's teacher will advise whether they are ready join the groups. Typically a child of 8 to 10 years with a year or two of playing would be ready to join.

Adults

Adult students are invited to join our Tuesday night class/Slow session which aims to develop the skills required for group playing in a session scenario. It provides a bridge for the intermediate student between the class situation and full session playing.
