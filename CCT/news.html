Children’s Open Trad Session at the Ark 26th & 27th January 2013

Children aged 8 to 12 who can play a tune or two can drop in to the free open trad sesúin in Ark in Temple Bar led by the young musicians of Ceoltóirí Chluain Tarbh. This is being run in association with Tradfest. It takes place Saturday 26th and Sunday 27th of January. Time: 12:00-14:30. Families are invited to come, watch and enjoy! See the Tradfest website for more information.

Ceoltóirí Chluain Tarbh at the Temple Bar Trad 26th & 27th January 2013

Temple Bar Trad is taking place from January 22nd to 27th in Dublin's Temple Bar district. As part of the festival Ceoltóirí Chluain Tarbh are performing on Saturday Jan 26th at 2:00 PM (under 18s) and 4:00 PM (over 18s) on the Cois Life stage. It's a free event and all are welcome! Meanwhile our under 12 musicians are leading the Open Trad session over in the Ark on Saturday 26th and Sunday 27th of January from 12:00 to 14:30
